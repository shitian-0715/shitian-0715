01 · Profile

Biography

Tian Shi received the B.Sc. degree in information and computing science and the M.Sc. degree in mathematics from Guilin University of Electronic Technology, Guilin, China, in 2019 and 2023, respectively.

He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering with Sun Yat-sen University, Guangzhou, China. His research interests include stochastic geometry, cooperative UAV communications, wireless network performance analysis, and complex-network synchronization.

02 · Focus

Research Interests

01

UAV Communications

Cooperative UAV communications, aerial networking, and deployment strategies for reliable wireless coverage and connectivity.

02

Stochastic Geometry

Spatial modeling and performance analysis of wireless networks, including coverage probability, distance distributions, and base-station deployment.

03

Complex Networks

Synchronization and control in networked systems, including directed hypergraphs, simplicial complexes, and higher-dimensional topological signals.
